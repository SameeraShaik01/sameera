From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Samajavaragamana is a 2023 Indian Telugu-language comedy drama film directed by Ram Abbaraju, from a story written by Bhanu Bogavarapu.[2]It is produced by Razesh Danda, under the banners, Ak Entertainments and Hasya Movies. It stars Sree Vishnu, Reba Monica John, Naresh, Srikanth Iyyengar, Vennela Kishore, Sudarshan, and Devi Prasad.[3]
